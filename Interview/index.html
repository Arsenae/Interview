E- What do you see yourself doing after you finish this program?

C- Getting a job that helps me get permanent residency. Really, I need to work at least one year in the field I graduated from to get the PR. After that maybe studying something related with CGI or video games.

E- What excites you about design?

E- What worries you about design?

C- Not fulfilling the clients or my colleague's expectations.

E- Can design change the world?

C- It already has, I mean, everything is different from what it was years ago, and I mean like 10 years ago, not the 20th century. Besides, everyday there are new amazing ideas that combined with the fast-technological development can change the world.
